Cracked Black Pepper Salmon

Description

This salmon recipe is simple to follow and provides brief direction on how to prepare and cook cracked black pepper salmon with freshly ground pepper.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup ground black pepper
  • 2 salmon fillets or 2 salmon steaks
  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil

Directions

  1. Spread ½ the black pepper on a plate.
  2. Smear 1 tsp olive oil over one side of the salmon fillets.
  3. Lay the fillet, oiled side down in the pepper.
  4. Lift and put on a baking sheet, peppered side up.
  5. Repeat the process with the second fillet.
  6. Bake at 350°F or 180°C for around 20 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily.
